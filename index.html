Welcome to MimicLabs! A large-scale initiative that aims to understand how extensive robotics datasets should be collected and employed to effectively enhance imitation learning for robots.


Check out our study paper, where we tackle this issue from the ground up - exploring the influence of various factors on the effectiveness of robotic imitation learning, by creating customized large-scale datasets in simulation.
